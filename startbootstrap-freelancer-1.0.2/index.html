FAQ

How do I place an order?

Please email us for a quote using the Contact Us form and specify the information requested. We will then reply within the next 24 hours with a total cost.


Do you build bespoke beds?

Yes we do have a bespoke service but this is not always available. Please contact us by using the enquiry form to find out more information.

What size mattress do the beds take?

Our beds are made to standard British sizes. We make them for;
-Junior size: 1400mmx700mm
-Single size: 1900mmx900mm

Please contact us if you have a special requirement.

NOTE: For safety reasons the maximum depth of the mattress used should be 200mm.

What are the overall measurements for the beds?

Junior with stairs at the side are 2000mm long, 800mm wide and 1200mm high.
Junior with stairs at the front is 1450mm long, 1318mm wide and 1200mm high.

Standard with stairs at the side are 2500mm long 1000mm wide and 1200mm high.
Standard with stairs at the front are 2000mm long 1518mm wide and 1200mm high.

The head height inside a standard playhouse is 850mm from the floor to the underneath of the bed.

The extended playhouse has 1200mm headroom underneath the bed.

What age group are the beds for?

This differs from child to child depending on their height but on average:
-Junior: 3-6 year olds
-Standard: 6+ Please note that it is generally recommended that the top bunk is only used by children who are 6+

How easy is it to assemble?

We pride ourselves on the simplicity of assembling our beds, but if you do get stuck we have a tutorial video that will explain it all in detail for you.

We also provide an full installation service for £30.

What areas do you deliver to?

Our delivery service covers mainland England.

We can deliver to Scotland but there is an additional fee (subject to area and availability).

Can I have the stairs at the opposite end?

The stairs can be placed either side of the bed and either side on the front of the bed.

Can you choose your own colour?

Yes as long as it's in the Dulux range.

Are the beds already painted?

Yes, for an additional fee we can paint the bed in a colour of your choice as long as it's in the Dulux range.

How long does it take for delivery?

Once payment has cleared it takes approximately 4-5 weeks for a painted bed.

Payment Plan

1. You pay a 20% deposit (administration fee) as the first payment into the plan. This is non refundable if you cancel the order at a later stage.

2. Subsequently you add further amounts to the plan. You choose the amount you pay with a minimum of £30 each time. We will confirm each payment in writing.

3. When you have reached 80% of the order value we will schedule production of your bed and arrange a convenient delivery date based on when you expect to have made all payments.

4. When 100% of the order value has been paid we will confirm an actual delivery date with you.

5. At any time you can cancel the order and we will repay the full amount you have paid excluding the 20% deposit which will be retained as an administration fee.

6. Please note that no interest is payable on this payment plan.

7. Payment can be made either by a bank transfer as follows:
